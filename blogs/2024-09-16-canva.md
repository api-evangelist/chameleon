This Canva whiteboard tour is straightforward yet lively, gently nudging users to start their creative journey. It starts with a playful "drag an element" nudge, making the user feel like they're casually playing with ideas rather than diving into work. The guided steps focus on collaboration, navigation, and time management without overwhelming users with jargon. It’s simple, interactive, and highlights the core functionalities, making it feel like you’re taking a tour with a friendly guide rather than a robotic checklist—perfect for turning first-timers into whiteboard pros.
